Who We Are

ShonkuWeb is a modern technology agency dedicated to building smart, scalable, and impactful digital solutions for small and medium businesses. We blend creativity with engineering excellence to craft products that move fast, feel effortless, and perform reliably.

Inspired by Professor Shonku

Our name pays homage to Professor Shonku, the brilliant fictional scientist from Satyajit Ray’s universe. His spirit of experimentation, bold thinking, and relentless innovation guides our approach to product strategy, design systems, and technical delivery.
